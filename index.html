Rasa: Taste of Substance


Virya: Active Principle


Vipaka: Final Transformation


Guna: Three qualities influencing all creation: sattva, rajas and tamas. Sattvic quailities imply essence, reality, consciousness, purity, and clarity of perception. All movement and activity are due to rajas. Tamas brings darkness, inertia, heaviness, and materalistic attitudes. There is a constant interplay among these three gunas in all creation.
